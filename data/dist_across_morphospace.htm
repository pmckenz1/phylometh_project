Appendix B (Fig. B1). Representation of six floral traits distributed across samples within the two-dimensional morphospace created by the first two PC Axes. Quantitative traits correlate most strongly with PC1; (A) circle size = relative log corolla tube length. (B) circle size = relative beak length. Binary traits correlate strongly with PC1; (C) nectar presence (black filled) vs. absence (open). (D) Bee orientation, sternotribic (black filled), nototribic (open). Discrete traits varied along both axes. (E) Flower color:outer color = primary color, inner color = secondary color. (F) Pollen placement positions: VA = ventral abdomen, VT = ventral thorax, DA = dorsal abdomen, DT = dorsal thorax, T-lateral = side of thorax lateral, A-lateral = side of abdomen, hindleg.
